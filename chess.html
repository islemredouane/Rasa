About the Department

The Chess Department of our club is a hub for thinkers, strategists, and learners. We dive deep into the art of the game — exploring openings, tactics, and endgames that sharpen the mind and strengthen decision-making skills. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced player, this is the place to grow, compete, and enjoy the game.



On the page below, you’ll find a selection of chess tutorials, interactive puzzles, and strategy guides designed to help you improve your play and challenge your thinking. We also bring a touch of fantasy and creativity to our club — hosting themed tournaments, fun challenges, and a welcoming community for all chess lovers.
